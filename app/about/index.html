Summary

I am a developer, specialising in web and iOS application development. I am experienced in using JavaScript to write dynamic client-side applications interacting with JSON APIs. I worked on many high-traffic projects for well-known brands, and excelled at working as a team to resolve complex technical issues.

Currently, AngularJS is my preferred client-side MVC framework as it is highly extensible and good at working with dynamic data. When writing CSS and HTML, I prefer using pre-processors such as SASS and HAML respectively due to their cleaner syntax.

I also have experience in writing native iOS applications using Swift, that run fast and memory efficient. I have a strong understanding of Ruby on Rails, having worked on various CMS and APIs.

I always test my code if I can, using BDD testing frameworks such as Jasmine to help write clean unit and E2E tests. Having worked at an agile development agency for many years, I am familiar with agile principals and continuous integration workflow.

I am good at evaluating technical approaches by considering the constraints and goals of a sprint. I am comfortable in engaging code review session with other team members.

I enjoy new challenges and learn about the best practices of modern application development.

Experience & Education

I am currently working at Red Ant (2009 - now) as a front-end developer. I predominantly work on Michelle Bridges' 12 Week Body Transformation - building their website and mobile application. I also spent a lot of time working on projects for Vibe Hotels, Beauty Heaven, Huggies, Yates, Selleys and Ratsak.

Before Red Ant, I worked at Channel Ten as a web designer (2008 - 09). My role was to design promotional materials and websites for popular TV shows such as Masterchefs and Australian Idol.

I graduated from University of Technology, Sydney with a Bachelor degree in Design, Visual Communication.
